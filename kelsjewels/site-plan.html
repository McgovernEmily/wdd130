KelsJewels Site Plan

Emily McGovern

WDD 130-04

Overview

Purpose

Have you ever wanted to wear beautiful hand-crafted jewelry with an easy-to-purchase website? This jewelry website will have an easy way of customizing and purchasing handmade jewelry. The navigation is easily accessible and easy to use. The jewelry will not be the only thing that catches your eye. This website will be filled with beautiful color patterns and fonts. Once you visit this website, you will be wanting to come back, not just for the jewelry but also for the ease of this website.

Audience

The intended audience would be men and women between the ages of 16-28. These young men or women are into fashion, ways to express themselves, and crystals. They will probably be looking for deals and would prefer to have cheaper prices. They will also be looking for an easy-to-use website with a more fantasy look. The audience will look for jewelry that has easy sizing and wonderful pictures to compare to. There will be different kinds of jewelry from bracelets to necklaces. The audience will most likely have access to this site through either a laptop or phone.
