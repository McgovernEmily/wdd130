Overview

Purpose

Have you ever wanted to wear beautiful hand-crafted jewelry with an easy-to-purchase website? This jewelry website will have an easy way of customizing and purchasing handmade jewelry. The navigation is easily accessible and easy to use. The jewelry will not be the only thing that catches your eye. This website will be filled with beautiful color patterns and fonts. Once you visit this website, you will be wanting to come back, not just for the jewelry but also for the ease of this website.

Audience

The intended audience would be men and women between the ages of 16-28. These young men or women are into fashion, ways to express themselves, and crystals. They will probably be looking for deals and would prefer to have cheaper prices. They will also be looking for an easy-to-use website with a more fantasy look. The audience will look for jewelry that has easy sizing and wonderful pictures to compare to. There will be different kinds of jewelry from bracelets to necklaces. The audience will most likely have access to this site through either a laptop or phone.

Content

Home page

People will want to come for the wonderful simple purchasing and navigation of this site. These people will find that there will not just be beautiful handmade jewelry, but there will be easy navigation and explanations of what this jewelry has to offer. The homepage will be a place to show off some of the newest jewelry we have gotten. This page will also have a small explanation of how this jewelry business started. The pictures will be showing off some of the jewelry that we have in stock and a picture of the founder of this jewelry business. Around the bottom of the page will have the buttons for the social media accounts that are for the jewelry business. This homepage will not just be a wonderful first look at the jewelry but will be a wonderful way to get to know the owner and the making of this fine jewelry.

Images for the Home page

Reviews and Questions

Some of the activities that our visitors will want to complete will be purchasing jewelry, wonderful pictures, reviews, helpful way to know what size is right for them, and a way to find their own fashion and taste in jewelry. The reviews and questions page will have some questions and reviews that were given by customers. Each question that is asked will be answered in full detail and each customer will be given the help they need. With some of these questions and reviews, we will have pictures and examples of our finest jewelry. The reviews and questions page will not just be wonderful answers to questions but will be easy to read and navigate through the reviews and questions.

Images for the Page 2

Jewelry

People are going to come to this website to find homemade jewelry and be able to find things that are their style. The other page will be pictures of some of the jewelry that we will be selling. Each picture will be given its own space, with a wonderful explanation about what is on this piece of jewelry. It will also show the price of what the producer wants the customer to pay for the piece of jewelry. I would like to try and add a link to each picture, to take them to another page to show a bigger picture. It will not just show the explanation of the piece and how much it will cost, but there will be buttons that will give the customers that opportunity to buy the piece of jewelry. These customers will not just come to this page for the beautiful homemade jewelry, but to find that the simplicity of navigating and shopping will be worth the experience.
