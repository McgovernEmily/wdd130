FAQ

What is Your Return/Exchange Policy?

Kels Jewelry wants you to love your new bracelet. If you do need to return an item we are here to help you. For returns, we do ask you to contact us within 30 days of receiving your purchase. We will gladly accept any returns and will offer you a full refund on your non-custom item. Items returned after 30 days will not be returnable. If there are any wear and noticeable brakes in the jewelry, there will be fees. All custom orders will not be returnable.

I Just Got My Bracelet But it Doesn't Fit. What Do I Do?

If your bracelet does not fit, please contact us. It will take approximately two to three weeks to complete the sizing repairs.

Do You Do Custom Work?

Yes! We love to do custom work, so please do not hesitate to ask about going customary.

How Much Does it Cost to Go Custom?

For a semi-customary job, which includes designs that are slightly altered from our original designs, the minimum is about $30. For a full customary job, the minimum is about $35. Please contact us if you are wanted any customary work done. We will gladly accept requests.
